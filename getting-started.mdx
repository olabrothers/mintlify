# **Getting Started**

**Sip** is a design tool that lets you collect, organize, and edit colors. It also allows you to share colors with others and link them across the tools in your workflow.

---

## **Minimum Requirements**

To use the latest version of Sip, you must be running **macOS Sonoma** or later.

---

## **Download Sip**

You can download a free 15-day trial of Sip [here](https://sipapp.io/downloading). After the trial ends, you’ll be prompted to purchase a license to continue using the app.

---

## **Purchasing Sip**

If Sip fits your workflow, you can purchase a license from our [store](https://sipapp.io/pricing). Each license includes **one year of updates**.  
Licensing is **per device**, so you only pay for what you use. The price per device varies depending on the number of devices you purchase for:
