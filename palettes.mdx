Palettes help you organize and manage your colors based on your project’s requirements and your team’s workflow. Grouping colors within a Palette mirrors how you might structure a color system.

## Create Palette

You can create a new palette in two ways: from the Main Window or directly from the Menu Bar.

### From the Main Window

<img className="block dark:hidden rounded-lg" src="/_images/pages/palettes/main-add-palette-light@2x.png" alt="main-add-palette" />
<img className="hidden dark:block rounded-lg" src="/_images/pages/palettes/main-add-palette-dark@2x.png" alt="main-add-palette" />

- Click the `+` button.

### From the Menu Bar

- Click the three-line menu (`☰`)
- Select **New Palette** from the dropdown.

<img className="block dark:hidden rounded-lg" src="/_images/pages/palettes/menu-add-palette-light@2x.png" alt="menu-add-palette" />
<img className="hidden dark:block rounded-lg" src="/_images/pages/palettes/menu-add-palette-dark@2x.png" alt="menu-add-palette" />

## Add Colors

Once you've created a palette, you can add colors to it by:

- Using the **Color Picker** while the Palette is selected.
- Dragging colors from other palettes into the new one.

<img className="block dark:hidden rounded-lg" src="/_images/pages/palettes/palette-add-color-light@2x.png" alt="palette-add-color" />
<img className="hidden dark:block rounded-lg" src="/_images/pages/palettes/palette-add-color-dark@2x.png" alt="palette-add-color" />

Palettes can be named, and you can add a description to help your team understand the palette's purpose, use cases, or project relevance.

## Grouping Colors

To group colors within a Palette:

- Select the colors you want to group.
- Open the contextual menu (right-click or use the menu icon).
- Select **Group** to create a named subgroup within the palette.

<img className="block dark:hidden rounded-lg" src="/_images/pages/palettes/palette-group-colors-light@2x.png" alt="palette-group-colors" />
<img className="hidden dark:block rounded-lg" src="/_images/pages/palettes/palette-group-colors-dark@2x.png" alt="palette-group-colors" />

## Share Palette

To share a palette:

- Click the **actions menu** (`•••`) in the top-right of a palette.
- Select **Export** and choose the desired format.

<img className="block dark:hidden rounded-lg" src="/_images/pages/palettes/share-palette-light@2x.png" alt="share-palette" />
<img className="hidden dark:block rounded-lg" src="/_images/pages/palettes/share-palette-dark@2x.png" alt="share-palette" />

### Export Formats

- **Sip Palette** – Re-importable into the app.
- **Adobe Swatch Exchange (ASE)** – For Adobe Creative Cloud.
- **Apple Color List (CLR)** – For macOS apps and Xcode.
- **JavaScript Object Notation (JSON)** – For developers.
- **Portable Document Format (PDF)** – For printable, shareable files.

These formats make it easy to use your color palettes across design, development, and documentation workflows.
