Supercharge your workflow with Sip’s **Shortcuts** feature—assign custom key combinations to essential actions like opening the **Contrast Checker**, **Picker**, or **Color Panel**—and get things done fast.

## Shortcuts

<img className="block dark:hidden rounded-lg" src="/_images/pages/shortcuts/shortcuts-main-light@2x.png" alt="shortcuts-main" />
<img className="hidden dark:block rounded-lg" src="/_images/pages/shortcuts/shortcuts-main-dark@2x.png" alt="shortcuts-main" />

The **Shortcuts** tab lets you manage and customize key combinations for a wide range of actions in Sip.

You can enable or disable shortcuts for:

- **Contrast Checker**
- **Picker**
- **Zoom & Size**
- **Color Dock**
- **Extensions**

To change a shortcut, click its field and press your preferred key combination.

<Tip>
	You can also reset all shortcuts to their default values by clicking
	<b>Reset Shortcuts</b>.
</Tip>

## Shortcuts List

| Action                    | Shortcut                       |
| ------------------------- | ------------------------------ |
| **Open Contrast Checker** | <kbd>⌃⌥⌘</kbd> + <kbd>O</kbd>  |
