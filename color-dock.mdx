The **Color Dock** makes your palettes easily accessible and unobtrusive.

It lives at the edge of your screen, always on top of other windows, so you can access your colors anytime, without losing focus on your work.

<img className="hidden dark:block rounded-lg" src="/_images/pages/dock/dock-left-dark@2x.png" alt="dock-left" />

## Adding Palettes to the Color Dock

You can choose which palettes appear in the Color Dock by enabling the **Add to Color Dock** option in each palette’s settings.

Simply click the icon in the top-right corner of a palette. You can display up to **10 palettes** in the Color Dock.

## Moving the Color Dock

To reposition the Color Dock:

- **Drag** it to a new location.
- When released, the Dock snaps to the nearest screen edge.

<Tip>
	To hide the Color Dock, drag it to the center of your screen. To show it again, go to <b>Preferences > Color Dock > Show Color Dock</b>.
</Tip>

## Actions in the Color Dock

Each palette in the Dock has an action icon (**•••**). Hover over it to reveal options:

- **Favorite** a palette.
- **Remove** a palette from the Color Dock.
- **Lock** a palette to prevent accidental edits.
- **Delete** a palette.
- **Open** the palette in the Editor.
