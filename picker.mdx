The **Picker** is your tool for selecting colors from anywhere on your screen. Once you pick a color, it’s automatically:

- Added to your **Color History**.
- Copied to your clipboard for instant use.

<img className="block h-auto w-full rounded-2xl dark:hidden" src="/_images/pages/picker/picker-light@2x.png" alt="picker" />
<img className="hidden h-auto w-full rounded-2xl dark:block" src="/_images/pages/picker/picker-dark@2x.png" alt="picker" />

## Accessing the Picker

There are several ways to open the Picker:

- Click the **Picker icon** in the **Menu Bar**.
- Use the **Picker icon** in the **Toolbar**.
- Select **Picker** from Sip's menu.
- Or use the shortcut: <kbd>^⌥⌘</kbd> + <kbd>P</kbd>.

## Customizing the Picker

Adjust the Picker’s appearance to match your workflow:

- Change the Picker’s **diameter**: <kbd>+</kbd> / <kbd>-</kbd>.
- Adjust the **grid size**: <kbd>⌥</kbd> + <kbd>+</kbd> / <kbd>⌥</kbd> + <kbd>-</kbd>.
- **Two-finger swipe** (trackpad or Magic Mouse) up/down to resize.
- Or use <kbd>⌥</kbd> + two-finger swipe for finer control.

## Moving the Picker

- **Drag the Picker** freely using your mouse or trackpad.
- For precise adjustments, use the keyboard:
  - Arrow keys: Move by 1 pixel.
  - Hold <kbd>⇧</kbd> + Arrow keys: Move by 10 pixels.

## Picking Multiple Colors & Shortcuts

While the Picker is open, hold the <kbd>⌘</kbd> key to access shortcuts:

- Pick a single color.
- Pick multiple colors.
- Check color contrast.

## Picker Plugins

When the Picker is open, hold the <kbd>⌘</kbd> key. If a supported app is detected, you’ll see a **Send to software** option, allowing you to send the selected color directly to that app.

<img className="block h-auto w-full rounded-2xl dark:hidden" src="/_images/pages/picker/picker-options-plugin-light@2x.png" alt="picker-options-plugin" />
<img className="hidden h-auto w-full rounded-2xl dark:block" src="/_images/pages/picker/picker-options-plugin-dark@2x.png" alt="picker-options-plugin" />

### Supported Software

Depending on the apps installed on your Mac, you can send colors directly to:

- **Illustrator**
- **InDesign**
- **Nova**
- **Photoshop**
- **Sketch**
- **VS Code**
- **Xcode**
